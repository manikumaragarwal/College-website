About Us

History, Mission, and Leadership

Accredited by HLC Top 50 Regional Colleges STEM Designated

Founded in 1968, Northbridge College blends liberal arts foundations with future-ready research and experiential learning. Our mission is to empower students to lead with integrity, innovate responsibly, and uplift the communities they serve.

We are guided by a vision of inclusive excellence, combining rigorous academics with mentorship, applied learning, and career readiness. Our values center on curiosity, collaboration, and community impact.
