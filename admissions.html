Apply online for UG and PG programs with scholarships and merit-based aid.

Admission Process

  1. Choose your program and review eligibility.
  2. Complete the online application form.
  3. Upload documents and pay application fee.
  4. Attend counseling/interview if applicable.
  5. Receive admission confirmation.
